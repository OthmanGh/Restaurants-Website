about us

what makes our restaurant special?

Located in the heart of zeitounay Bay Beirut, when brought to light in 2012 Babel Bay was destined to pay homage to Beirut, with a meticulous interior replicating the capital's shoreline laying on top of 7 sandstones symbolizing the 7 destructions which Beirut has faced.

Babel Bay is renowned for its cosmopolitan seafood dishes prepared with the outlet's progressive touch.
